Privacy Policy for Plantry

Effective Date: November 3, 2025

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

Quick Summary: Plantry is a 100% offline app. All your plant data stays on your device. We don't collect, upload, or share any of your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Plantry ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

2. Data Collection - WE COLLECT ZERO DATA

Plantry collects ZERO user data. We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any of your information on our servers or any third-party servers.

Plantry is designed to work completely offline. The App stores the following data exclusively and only locally on your device:

  • Plant Information: Names, strains, growing phases, and other plant-related data you enter
  • Grow Logs: Notes, measurements (pH, EC, temperature, humidity), watering schedules, and feeding data
  • Photos: Images you take or select from your device to document plant growth
  • Room & Equipment Data: Information about your grow rooms and hardware
  • App Settings: Your preferences such as language and theme (dark/light mode)

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no servers, there is no way for us to access or share your information.

The only way your data leaves your device is if you choose to:

  • Export a backup file using the app's export feature
  • Share a backup file via email, cloud storage, or messaging apps

6. Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export Data: Use the "Export Data" feature in Settings to create a complete backup
  • Import Data: Restore your data from a previous backup
  • Delete Data: Clear all data by resetting the database in Settings (Debug section)
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all data from your device

8. Children's Privacy

Plantry is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are under the required age, please do not use this app.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on:

  • Your device's security measures (lock screen, encryption)
  • Your backup file security (if you export data, store it securely)

We recommend:

  • Using a device lock screen (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, etc.)
  • Storing backup files in a secure location
  • Not sharing your backup files with untrusted parties
